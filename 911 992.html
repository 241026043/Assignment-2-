The Porsche 911 992

The 2025 Porsche 911 (992.2) brings several exciting updates, including the introduction of hybrid technology for the first time in the model's history. The Carrera GTS now features a 3.6-liter flat-six engine paired with Porsche's new T-Hybrid system. This system includes an electric exhaust gas turbocharger and a permanent magnet synchronous motor, contributing to a significant power boost. The Carrera GTS produces up to 398 kW (533 hp) and 610 Nm (450 lb-ft) of torque, enabling a 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph) time of just 3.0 seconds for the rear-wheel-drive version.
In terms of design, the 2025 911 maintains its iconic look with subtle refinements, such as new wheel designs, matrix LED headlights, and a revised rear end with a full-width LED light strip. The interior has also seen updates, including a 12.6-inch fully digital instrument cluster and a push-button starter replacing the traditional key or rotary dial. The base Carrera models also benefit from enhancements, like a slight power increase and a more efficient twin-turbo 3.0-liter flat-six engine. All 911 variants now come with rear-wheel steering, improving handling and stability.
